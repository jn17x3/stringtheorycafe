String Theory is a cafe for knitters and crocheters located in San Francisco. Our cafe not only offers coffee, tea, and pastries, but also yarn and knitting/ crochet materials. Customers can enjoy a cup of coffee while having a space to work on projects and interact with members of the knitting and crochet community.
